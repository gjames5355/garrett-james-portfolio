Tallyho!

Tired of making a visual schedule by hand? Look no further parents! Tallyho! makes the process easier without the manual labor. Users can create, update, delete, and check off event items on their schedule. Made with love - React.js, Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL, and JWT Token.

Demo GitHub

Chorerunner!

Chorerunner is a React chore application where Users can sign up for an account and assign chores to members of their families. The app allows users to make a game out of household chores, which will make them more likely to actually complete them! The user can assign point values to the tasks. The family member with the highest point total is the winner - React.js, Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL, and JWT Token.
